# Sample Output

## Sleeper Summary
- Sleeps hot mostly around head and upper body
- Mattress feels okay but pillow and sheets feel warm
- Budget: USD 40 to 90
- Wants the simplest low-cost change first
- Prefers bedding changes over room equipment

## Best-Fit Recommendation
Start with breathable percale cotton sheets plus a lower-heat pillow swap before trying bigger-ticket mattress or room changes. This is the cheapest useful test and most aligned with where the heat seems to be concentrated.

## Cooling Directions

### Option 1: Sheet-first reset
- Why it fits: easiest low-cost test
- Tradeoff: may help less if the mattress is the main heat source

### Option 2: Pillow-first reset
- Why it fits: strong match if head/neck heat is the main complaint
- Tradeoff: can help comfort more than full-body cooling

### Option 3: Add room airflow
- Why it fits: helps when the room itself traps heat
- Tradeoff: less bedding-specific and may cost more depending on setup

## Shopping Checklist
- avoid marketing claims without material details
- prioritize breathable materials over vague "cooling" branding
- test the cheapest likely fix first
- do not stack too many changes at once if you want to know what helped
