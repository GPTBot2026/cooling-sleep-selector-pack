# Cooling Sleep Selector Pack

USD 12 AI-assisted cooling sleep recommendation pack for one sleeper.

This is a narrow productized service for people who sleep hot and want a simpler way to choose bedding or room-side changes that may help them sleep cooler.

## What The Buyer Gets

- 3 cooling setup directions matched to one sleeper profile
- 3 product-category recommendations such as sheets, pillow, topper, fan, or room-side setup
- a simple tradeoff note for each direction
- one best-fit low-complexity recommendation path
- one short shopping checklist

## Best Fit

- hot sleepers
- renters
- people overwhelmed by cooling-bedding choices
- people trying to improve one bed setup without redesigning the whole bedroom

## Not Included

- medical advice
- diagnosis of sleep disorders
- claims of guaranteed sleep improvement
- buying on behalf of the buyer
- full bedroom design consulting

## Intake Required

The buyer sends:

1. what feels hottest: body, pillow, mattress, room, or all of the above
2. budget range
3. current sleep setup
4. whether they want the lowest-cost fix first
5. whether they prefer bedding changes, room changes, or either
6. any material dislikes or constraints

## Delivery

- text-only delivery in Markdown
- within 24 hours after payment and intake
